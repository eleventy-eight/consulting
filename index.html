Why Hire Me?

Expertise without the Geek Speak

With 20 years of experience in getting businesses online and making them money, I bring a keen eye for detail and advanced technical knowledge. And no, I'm not the anti-social techie stereotype.

I feel that using excessive technical jargon is a way to create confusion and mystify concepts in order to fuel an ego, it doesn't help business owners make important decisions on the future of their businesses.

I'm also totally self taught in web design and development, I've made some mistakes in my career and learnt from them. I'm a safe pair of hands because I've done a lot of trial and error over the years and know what works. You could benefit from my experiences.

I love problem-solving, and I'm good at it. Set me a challenge, and I'll find a way to solve it, or think my way around it.

Who am I?

Self-Taught, Proven Results

My career began when I was asked by a mortgage broker if I could build a website. I found myself going down a rabbit hole and wanting to learn how to code and manage websites. I spent months of my free time watching tutorials and experimenting with code.

I've never stopped learning.

I started managing a PHP forum for a friend, from that I landed a job at Lenovo in their EMEA department. I've worked for small agencies and did a stint at GAME PLC in their online division.

JMA Cleaning Solutions is a small business I've helped to gain more presence online and grow their business.

My main gig has been working for The Property Jungle where I've been responsible for all sorts of code and documentation. I've been in charge of new innovations and generally keeping websites running and making sure staff get what they need to complete projects.

You can read a little more about my career on LinkedIn.

How I Operate

Ask the Right Questions, and Listen

Asking the right questions and getting to understand more about your business is how I get results.
